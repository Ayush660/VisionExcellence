ABOUT LEAN SIX SIGMA

In simple terms, Lean and Six Sigma are two different continuous improvement programs that have been popularized among a variety of industries of all sizes over the past few decades. Lean is a quick win approach that helps in engaging the workforce and improving business processes by eliminating wasteful activities. The principles of Lean, when applied effectively, can make processes simple, efficient, smooth and faster for its stakeholders. On the other hand, Six Sigma is a fact-based, data-driven approach to systematically eliminate defects by reducing the part to part variation. A Trained team of professionals, who are belt holders use statistics and prevention methods extensively to tackle strategic and complex projects. Generally, both programs are highly successful and are commonly merged to take advantage of their strength.

We help organizations accomplish these benefits with Lean Six Sigma.
WHY DO YOU NEED LEAN AND SIX SIGMA?

Lean and Six Sigma initiatives when applied with the support of top management can derive tangible benefits to any organization. These two popular process improvement techniques can help organizations engage their employees to increase customer satisfaction through improved quality and quicker response times along with the following tangible benefits:

  1. Reduced labor costs and process times
  2. Improved cash flow and reduced inventory
  3. Reduced expenses
  4. Increased sales and improved customer satisfaction
  5. Reduced risks and potential failures
  6. Reduced environmental and improved community impact
